Projects
A gallery of several of the projects that I've worked on for my classes and clubs at USC. Click on a project for more details!

Coffee Grinder Redesign

A semester long project for my advanced mechanical design class that started with customer ethnography, which informed the development of customer needs and functional decomposition. After developing a few design variants, my group then developed a final product.

Hair Dryer DFMA Analysis

For my DFMA class, I designed a hair dryer, focusing on design for manufacturability and assembly and performed a DFMA analysis of the design.

Next Level Grip: Senior Design Project

A grip strength rehabilitation device for stroke survivors that I designed and prototyped for my senior design project.

Coffee Grinder Product Dissections

A teardown of a two different coffee grinders for my advanced mechanical design class to inform my coffee grinder redesign.

Marble Run SolidWorks Design

A marble run that I designed as the final project for my SolidWorks class.

Inspecula Speculum Redesign

A speculum redesign that I worked on as a member of my undergraduate medical device design team. The attached pitch deck was used in the finals of the USC Viterbi MEPC pitch competition

AthenaHacks: Materna Monitor

A maternal health monitor that my team designed for USC AthenaHacks Hackathon 2022. We won the hackathon's grand prize for our combined hardware and software design.

Car Air Vent Hand Sanitizer Dispenser

A car air vent hand sanitizer dispenser that I designed as the midterm project for my SolidWorks class.

Butterfleye Eye Drop Aid

My team's design for an eye drop dispensing aid for the 2019 UC Davis Makeathon. We won the "Most Potential" award for our final product design and cost analysis.
